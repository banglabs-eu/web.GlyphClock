About GlyphClock

GlyphClock is a timezone and language free time system. The 24-hour day is divided into 16 blocks of 90 minutes, each block with a unique emoji. Each 90 minute period has 3 sub-periods of 30 minutes, shown by displaying one, two, or three copies of the glyph.

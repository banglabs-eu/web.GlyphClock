About GlyphClock

We all live in and share the same time. The clock is always ticking, GlyphClock was made with the intent to separate us from the constant count.

The 24-hour day is divided into 16, 90 minute blocks. Each block is then divided into 30 minute blocks of 一, 二 and 三.

There are no time zones in GlyphClock, as we travel the world we develop new relationships to the symbols. 🦋 may go from being the middle of the night to when we wake up. The 🕰️ will show you the current GlyphClock.

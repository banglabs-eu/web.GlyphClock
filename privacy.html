Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 March 2026

Overview

GlyphClock is built with privacy as a core principle. The GlyphClock Apple Watch widget and web app do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

GlyphClock does not collect any personal information. The app reads the current UTC time from your device to calculate which glyph block to display. This time data is used locally and is never sent to any server.

Analytics & Tracking

GlyphClock does not use analytics, tracking, cookies, or any third-party services. There are no ads.

Network Usage

The Apple Watch widget operates entirely offline. It does not make any network requests.

Data Storage

The web app stores your theme preference and language selection locally in your browser using localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

Children’s Privacy

GlyphClock does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at bang-labs.eu.

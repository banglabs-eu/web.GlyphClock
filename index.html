About GlyphClock

GlyphClock is focus over precision and a slower rhythm to the day. The day is divided into 16 blocks of 90 minutes, each represented by a unique emoji glyph. Time progresses in 30-minute phases, shown by displaying one, two, or three copies of the glyph. The time is the same everywhere in the world, but the meaning of each glyph becomes personal. A butterflies may come to mean wake up for one person and a well earned break for another.

You can set a focus text, which stays visible beneath the clock to remind you what you want to be doing during the current block.

Also available as a Firefox add-on.
